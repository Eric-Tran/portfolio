About Me

My passion for web development began when I was working as an account manager at a digital media marketing agency. I helped clients create targeted advertisements and leads that closely matched their goals.

While collaborating with our resident web developer, I admired the work he produced for our clients' websites and quickly became interested in web development.

After spending over 75+ hours per week learning, researching, and building full-stack applications at Coding Dojo, I am currently learning new technologies such as ReactJS. I am ready to join a company and expand my professional experience in web development.
