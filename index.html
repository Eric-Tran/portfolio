About Me

Before learning how to code, I was an account manager at a digital media marketing agency working with my clients to bring targeted advertisements and leads that closely matched my clients' goals.

While collaborating with our resident web developer, I admired the work he produced for our clients' websites and quickly became interested in web development.

I believe there are many similarities between marketing and web development that will help me transition towards a web development career; both careers require and encourage team collaboration, organization, having a creative mindset, attending to details, and striving in a deadline driven environment.
